## 1. Scoring model

Total score = relevance score + publication score.

- Relevance screening is performed before publication-tier scoring.
- Preprints receive a uniform publication score of 3.
- Journal articles receive a tiered publication score according to the 2025 Journal Impact Factor table.
- Journal-level metrics are used for prioritisation only and do not replace article-level methodological appraisal.
